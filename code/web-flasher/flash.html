photodiode flasher - help

configure your prototype badge here!

first, make sure your badge is powered on and displaying the photodiode flasher screen. you'll need to power it over usb.

enter your configuration details in the fields provided!

next, position your badge so the photodiode area is aligned with the outlined box on this page.

hit "Start" to begin the flashing process. the photodiode will read the configuration data and write it to your badge. this can take anywhere from a few seconds to a minute depending on the data size.

once the process is complete, your badge will be configured with the new settings. reset your badge to apply the changes.
